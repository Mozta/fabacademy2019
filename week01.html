The sketch //

My sketch represents the idea of my final project, which is a smart desk with adjustable height.

I have 2 desktop prototypes proposal, prototype 1 has a simple but resistant design, which facilitates the movement that this will have to regulate the height. The prototype 2 has a more sophisticated and elegant design but that complicates the movement of the height.
